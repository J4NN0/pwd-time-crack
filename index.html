Why This Website?

I recently came across an article discussing the time required to crack passwords. While the concept is fascinating, it can be difficult to grasp.

Inspired by a wordlist generator I developed during my university years (and adapted here for demonstration purposes), this website aims to provide a practical example to help users understand the ethical implications of password security.

Please note that this website demonstrates ethical hacking for educational purposes only. In real-world scenarios, attackers often target hashed passwords and may use pre-generated wordlists or hash tables to accelerate the cracking process.
